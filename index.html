What Makes Us Different?

Here's just a few of the things we do different!

Pricing Strategy

We use a dynamic pricing software to create a pricing strategy that maximizes revenue by monitoring market trends of occupancy, events, and nightly rates of competition. On top of that, we additionally adjust price according to location, holidays, and events in the area!

Algorithmic Optimization

We understand how the different booking platform's algorithms work and how to get your property to the first page of search results! We'll do a weekly check and give a report of where your property ranks in the search results!

Vet Cleaners

Cleanliness is the most important part of getting 5-star reviews and having an enjoyable experience for your guests! On top of vetting our cleaners, we personally visit the property after every cleaning to make sure the job is getting done as it should be and so that you don't get any surpise, bad reviews!

    About

    A little bit about us!

    • May 2022

      Seeing An Opportunity

      Back in 2021, Cake worked as a real estate agent for a local brokerage in Utah County. Although it was a great job, he knew he wanted to transition into something different while still staying within the real estate industry.

    • July 2022

      Henline Co-Host Was Born

      That's when he was introduced to the idea of Airbnb "Co-hosting." After finding a mentor, he soon after created his own short-term rental management company called "Henline Co-Host."

    • November 2022

      Transition to Ha'le Rentals

      After spending some time in the management game, the company needed a re-branding to put emphasis on helping guests feel at "home" during their stay. Thus, "Ha'le Rentals" was born. Ha'le is hawaiian for home.

    • Current

      Where We Are Now

      That brings us to now. Though still small, Ha'le Rentals manages a 2.5MM+ portfolio and is growing with each new month. We hope you give us a call and decide to work with us!
